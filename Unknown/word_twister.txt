Twist My Words
============
Take an input English sentence and tokenize it.
Define a set of legal transformations rules.
Transform (i.e. reorder) the tokens based on a random rule
Replace the tokens with their original words/phrases.
Example:
	Given: 	Yesterday, we went to ShopRite to buy a turkey.
	Output:	We went to ShopRite yesterday to buy a turkey.
			OR
		To buy a turkey, we went to ShopRite yesterday.
			OR
		Yesterday, to buy a turkey, we went to ShopRite.
